Welcome to the Campus Center!

The Campus Center Complex is the student union of the University of Hawai`i at Manoa.

We offer the university community and the public many options for meeting, dining, and entertainment, as a primary venue for programs and events in the heart of the Manoa Campus. With facilities throughout the complex (Campus Center, Hemenway Hall, and Warrior Recreation Center), our goal is to enrich campus life and education by creating an environment where individuals can relax, study, be entertained, and challenge themselves.

Come relax and take a load off in the comforts of the Campus Center Computer Lab and Lounge. You can read, study or relax in an inviting and comfortable environment. Newspapers, e-mail service, and computer access are also available.

To use the CC Lab and Lounge, you will need to have: a valid and unexpired UH Student/Faculty & Staff ID card, or a valid unexpired UH username and password. The CC Lab and Lounge is open to students and faculty & staff from the UH system.
